Check In - Two apps makes simple

Check In is a most efficient way to perform attendance tracking for any type of institution using your android device. It is the software application you need to track your employee’s attendance and manage time sheets.


Check In Admin app helps to track and manage attendance of employees. This app makes sure no more writing down attendance information of students or employees as It’s all managed within the admin app.


Check In have Two types of app, one is Check In Tracker app and another one is Check In Admin app to track employees attendance through mobile from anywhere.
